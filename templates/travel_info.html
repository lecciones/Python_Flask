Travel Information

Everything you need to know before your flight

🛄 Baggage Policy

Each passenger is allowed one carry-on bag and one personal item. Checked baggage allowances depend on ticket class and destination. Make sure your baggage meets airline size and weight limits.

🛂 Travel Documents

Ensure you bring a valid passport, visa (if required), and boarding pass. Some destinations may require additional travel permits or vaccinations. Always check government travel guidelines before departure.

⏰ Airport Arrival Time

Arrive at least 2 hours before domestic flights and 3 hours before international flights. This allows enough time for check-in, security screening, and boarding procedures.

💺 Seat Selection

Passengers may select their preferred seat during booking or through the manage booking page. Additional fees may apply depending on seat location and travel class.

Travel Tips

Check Weather

Review the weather conditions of your destination before traveling.

Pack Smart

Pack light and keep important documents in your carry-on bag.

Stay Updated

Monitor flight updates for possible delays or gate changes.
